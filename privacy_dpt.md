Privacy Policy for Dead Pixel Tester

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Dead Pixel Tester application ("the App"). The App was built from the ground up with strict privacy and security principles. Our philosophy is simple: what happens on your device, stays on your device.

2. Data Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App functions 100% offline and operates entirely locally on your device hardware. We do not use any analytics software, crash reporting tools, or hidden tracking scripts to monitor how you use the App.

3. Device Permissions

The App requires absolutely zero system permissions to function. It does not request, nor does it have access to, your internet connection, camera, microphone, local storage, contacts, or location.

The App only utilizes standard Android UI commands (specifically FLAG_KEEP_SCREEN_ON and WindowInsetsControllerCompat) to manage screen brightness and hide system navigation bars temporarily during testing.

4. Third-Party Access and Advertising

Because the App does not connect to the internet, it is impossible for it to transmit data to third-party services.

No Ads: The App is completely ad-free. There are no third-party advertising networks integrated into the code.

No Data Selling: Since no data is ever collected, no data is ever sold, rented, or shared with external entities.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is entirely safe for all ages. It does not contain any mature content, does not connect to the internet, and does not collect any personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. However, because the App is fundamentally designed to be a local, offline utility, our core commitment to zero data collection will not change. Any updates to this document will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
